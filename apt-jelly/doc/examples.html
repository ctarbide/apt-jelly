Examples

Too many words. Just show me.

These are some scripts that are used in the unit tests. They all output a properties file (and the tests check the properties they output). You may want to checkout the source and see what the tests are doing, too.

  • testBasicExample.jelly (outputs a bunch of interesting properties of a source base)
  • testForAllPackages.jelly (iterates through all the packages of a source base)
  • testForAllTypes.jelly (iterates through all types of annotated by a specific annotation)
  • testPropertyName.jelly (utilizes the decorations on a method for property naming)
  • testIncludeSuperclasses.jelly (iterates through a bunch of methods including superclasses)
  • testIfHasAnnotation.jelly (example for testing whether a declaration has an annotation and for outputting an annotation value)
